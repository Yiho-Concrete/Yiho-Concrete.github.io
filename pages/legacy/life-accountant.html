Hola World

The world is like a jungle
Full of mysteries.

Life is limited, and that is why it's so precious.

Life accountant visulises the number of days, weeks or months in your own perspective. Life is never as simple as a straight line, but the speed that all of us travel around the sun is more or less constant. What will I become I once again go around the sun? The fuel may be a bit less, but the precious moments that we store in the box of memory grows. Mark those memories with the special colours on the Life Accountant, and continue the space travel. After all, there are so many things to see.
